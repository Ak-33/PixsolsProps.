These are the dual-wielding pistols used by the loose protagonist of the Risk of Rain games, Commando. In order to get the model, I ripped them from Risk of Rain 2 using Ninja Ripper. I printed them on my Ender 3 SE using PLA filament. I printed them in two parts each and glued them together with super glue.
